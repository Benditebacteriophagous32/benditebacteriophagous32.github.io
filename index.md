## 🛠️ System requirements

Relay requires macOS version 13 or newer. The app functions correctly on both Intel-based Macs and Apple Silicon processors. Ensure you have at least 200MB of free storage space before you start the installation process. An active internet connection remains necessary to sync new messages.

## 💎 Key features

Relay offers several improvements over the standard web version of Facebook Messenger.

### Private chat history
The application saves your chat history directly to your hard drive. This allows you to search through past conversations without waiting for the network to load data. The app uses an encrypted SQLite database to store this information safely.

### Live translation
The built-in translation tool converts incoming messages into your preferred language in real time. You do not need to copy and paste text into external translation websites. Select the language in the settings menu to enable this feature for your active chats.

### Native performance
Because Relay uses native code, it runs faster than web-based messaging clients. It consumes less memory and reduces the load on your processor. The interface utilizes Liquid Glass styling to provide a modern, translucent look that blends with your desktop wallpaper.

### Automatic updates
Relay checks for the latest features and security improvements every time you open the app. The Sparkle framework manages these updates in the background. You receive a notification whenever a new version waits for installation.

## ⚙️ Configuration settings

Open the settings menu by clicking the Relay icon in your menu bar. 

- **Account Management:** Link your existing Facebook account to begin receiving messages. Relay uses the real protocol to communicate with Meta servers securely.
- **Visual Themes:** Toggle between light and dark modes to match your system preferences. 
- **Notification Control:** Choose how you receive alerts for new messages. You can silence specific conversations or hide message previews to maintain your privacy.
- **Storage Path:** Choose where the app saves your local database files. 

## 🛡️ Privacy and security

Relay respects your data. The software does not track your online activity for advertising purposes. Your conversation history stays on your computer. The app connects directly to the messaging servers using encrypted tunnels to protect your identity from unauthorized third parties.

## 📈 Troubleshooting tips

If you encounter issues during setup, try these steps:

1. **Verify your OS version:** Click the Apple symbol in the top left corner, then select About This Mac. Ensure you use macOS 13 or newer.
2. **Check network access:** Ensure your firewall does not block the application. 
3. **Restart the app:** Quit the app completely and restart it from your Applications folder.
4. **Reinstall:** If the app behaves incorrectly, delete the current version and download a fresh copy from the link provided above. 
5. **Clear your cache:** If the interface fails to render properly, navigate to the local support folder and delete the cache files created by the app. The next launch will generate fresh, clean files.
